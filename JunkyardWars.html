Junkyard Wars

Junkyard Wars was a challenge of Creativity. Could you come up with an idea that could solve a problem from Junk?

With Bottles, straws, a rolling pin, wood, spoons, and metal rods, we came up with a Hydropowered car on a track that will drip water onto plants beneath it.
We made the wheels out of the rolling pin. The spoons and a bottle cap became our gears. The metal rod + Straws were our axles. And of course the water was held in the bottles.

Overall, our car was relatively successful, however due to gluing issues, our wheels & axles did not align correctly overnight resulting in misaligned wheel rotations.
